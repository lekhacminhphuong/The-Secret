To obtain this filter, you need to access to VSCO Cam application. After choosing the photo that you want to edit, you select C3 filter with the level 12, and then doing some extra edit steps such as changing the level of Exposure to -3, the level of Contrast to -1.5, the level of Clarity to +3, the level of Saturation to +1, the level of Hightlight to +2.6, the level of Tint to +1, and finally the level of Grain to +4.
