    To obtain this filter:
  • You need to access to VSCO Cam application.
  • After choosing the photo that you want to edit, you select C8 filter with the level 12
  • Doing some extra edit steps such as changing:
      The level of Exposure to +1
      The level of Contrast to +1
      The level of Clarity to +0.7
      The level of Saturation to +1.3
      The level of Shadows to +2
      The level of Temperature to -0.3
